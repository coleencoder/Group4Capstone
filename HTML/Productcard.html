Polymyxin B sulfate - bacitracin zinc is a combination of antibiotics used to treat certain types of infections caused by bacteria. The topical ointment can be used to treat certain skin infections such as infected wounds, burns, skin grafts, boils, and acne. The eye ointment is used to treat some types of eye infections such as conjunctivitis. All preparations work by killing the bacteria that cause these infections.
