About the Website

The proponents recognize and uphold the importance of access to medicine, especially in a time when the threat of the pandemic is still apparent. It is important that Filipinos get their medicine without sacrificing their health. There is also the urgency of acquiring medicine. Alternatives must quickly be available in case the nearby pharmacies do not offer the medicine needed. Customers should not need to hop from one pharmacy to another to check the availability of the medicine they need. It would lead to their exhaustion, frustration, and potentially expose them to people who are infected by the virus. While medicine can be bought online, the proponents thought the experience of doing so still has room for improvement.

With these concerns in mind, the proponents came up with an online portal system that would allow customers to view the availability of medicines in the pharmacies in Marikina City. They can check where their desired medicine is available for purchase. The customers can also buy it from the system. It will streamline the process of finding and buying medicine, bringing convenience and safety to customers.
